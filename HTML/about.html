About myself

I'm Shivanjali! I am a UX/UI Designer with a background in accounting and economics, trained and certified through UC Irvine's UX/UI program. I began my career in public accounting, serving clients, crunching numbers and developing as a professional. At the time, I was not aware of the field of UX Design, however I deeply empathized with my colleagues and clients when interacting with our softwares brought frustration. I found myself becoming hyper-aware of the interfaces I interacted with on a daily basis and I grew passionate about improving user experiences in order to allow users to think less and do more. I transitioned to UX/UI Design to pursue a future where data, creativity, and empathy meet.
